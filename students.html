Solidarity in the time of COVID-19

About This Program: CodingNomads #StayAtHome Mentorship Program is a free service that pairs voluntary mentors with people who lost their jobs due to COVID-19, and who would like to explore new skills. This is an experimental program, and we are in the preliminary phase of rolling it out. The program leverages our network and community to connect skilled mentors with motivated learners. Our goal and our hope is to help people use their time at home for education, to emerge from this crisis with more connections and new skills.

Why We're Launching This Program Now: Millions of people worldwide are facing dire straits due to workplaces closing during the COVID-19 pandemic. Self-isolation currently seems to be the most effective way to slow the outbreak, lighten the load on our public health system, and save lives. However, self-isolation forces many people to make an impossible choice between staying home and forgoing necessary income, versus potentially spreading the virus and continuing to go to work. Many essential workers are continuing to go to work and are helping to keep our communities as stable and safe as possible. Many others were not even given the chance to make that choice, and are now unemployed and without income due to lock-downs and the unfolding economic crisis.

Who Is Eligible For This Program: You qualify for this program if you have lost work, been laid off, or fired due to the COVID-19 pandemic, and you want to stay at home in self-isolation. Please apply below, we'd like to help you! If you are interested in becomming a mentor, please click here instead.

Learn Valuable Skills For Free: If you qualify for this program and you are considering a career transition into tech, apply below for 8 weeks of free access to one or more courses on CodingNomads Online Learning Platform. As well as the opportunity to be paired with a volunteer mentor. You can choose to learn Python - one of the most in-demand and easiest to learn programming languages, or Java - one of the most widely used programming languages in many industries. If Java or Python are not your cup of tea, please let us know below what you're interested in studying and we'll do our best to find the right mentor for you!

Make The Best Of This Time: Increasing your technical skills will not solve the economic and societal problems we are currently facing, but it can help to put yourself in a more stable position. Tech jobs are still some of the best paid and high in-demand positions in many places around the world, and they can give you the opportunity to work remotely. Being able to create value remotely and getting paid even during a situation like the current COVID-19 lock-downs is a privilege that we would like to make as available as possible to affected workers.
Disclaimer: At CodingNomads we know the value and importance of mentorship, so we aim to pair you with a mentor to elevate your progress and keep you accountable. Remember that your mentor will be volunteering their time. The ongoing availability of this program depends on each mentor and student. The content, schedule and length of each program will be subject to availability and modification.
