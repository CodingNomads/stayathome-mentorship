Solidarity in the time of COVID-19

About the Program
CodingNomads #StayAtHome Mentorship Program is a free service that pairs people who lost their jobs due to COVID-19 with volunteer mentors to learn new skills. This is an experimental program, and we are in the preliminary phase of rolling it out. The program leverages our network and community to connect skilled mentors with motivated learners. Our goal and our hope is to help everyone make the best use of their time at home, and emerge from this crisis stronger, better educated, and more united.

Who is eligible for this program
You qualify for this program if you have lost work, been laid off, or fired due to the Covid-19 pandemic, and you are staying at home in self-isolation. If you are interested in becomming a mentor, please Click Here.

Addressing Economic Instability
Millions of people worldwide are facing dire straits due to workplaces closing during the COVID-19 pandemic. Self-isolation is the most effective way to contain the outbreak, lighten the load on our public health system, and save lives. Yet this leaves many people having to choose between potentially spreading the pandemic vs. forgoing necessary income, while many others are now unemployed due to lock-downs.

Learn Valuable Skills For Free
If you qualify for this program and you are considering a career transition into tech, apply below for free access to CodingNomads course curriculum. You can choose to learn Python - one of the most in-demand and easiest to learn programming languages, or Java - one of the most widely used programming languages in many industries. In addition, we're also building a community of experienced mentors with a wide variety of skills across the design and development spectrum. So please let us know below what you'd like to learn and we'll do our best to find the right mentor for you!

Take Advantage of this Time
Increasing your technical skills will not solve the economic and societal problems we are currently facing, but it can help to put yourself in a more stable position. Tech jobs are still some of the best paid and high in-demand positions in many places around the world, and they can give you the opportunity to work remotely. Being able to continue creating value and getting paid even during a situation like the current COVID-19 lock-downs is a privilege that we would like to share with affected workers.
Disclaimer
At CodingNomads we know the value and importance of mentorship, so we aim to pair you with a mentor to elevate your progress and keep you accountable. Remember that your mentor will be volunteering their time. The ongoing availability of this program depends on each mentor and student. The content, schedule and length of each program will be subject to availability and modification. Our intention is to emerge from this crisis better and smarter than we were before, and we're here to do everything we can to help.
