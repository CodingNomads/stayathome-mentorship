Solidarity in the times of COVID-19

Economic Instability: Tens of thousands of people find themselves in dire straits through their workplaces being closed down due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Self-isolation is the most effective way to contain the outbreak, lighten the load on our public health system, and save lives. Many economically vulnerably people who are wanting to be respectful, however, find themselves needing to choose between potentially spreading the pandemic or forgoing necessary income. Many don't even have that choice and were simply laid off.

Learn To Program For Free: Increasing your technical skills will not solve the economic and societal problems we are currently facing, but it can help to put yourself in a more stable position. Tech jobs are still some of the best paid and high in-demand positions in many places around the world, and they can give you the opportunity to work remotely. Being able to continue creating value and getting paid even during a situation like the current COVID-19 lock-downs is a privilege that we would like to share with affected workers.

If you lost your job due to the effects of this pandemic and are staying at home in self-isolation and you are considering a career transition into tech, apply here for free access to our course curriculum to learn Python, one of the most in-demand and easiest to learn programming languages.
At CodingNomads we know the value and importance of mentorship for learning to code, so we will aim to pair you with a pro-bono mentor to keep you accountable and check in with you while you study this new skill. Please keep in mind that mentors will give their free time to you, and we can't promise to keep this going, since it depends on the availability of individual people. But we'll do our best.
