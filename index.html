Solidarity in the time of COVID-19

About the Program
CodingNomads #StayAtHome Mentorship Program is a free service that pairs people who lost their jobs due to COVID-19 with volunteer mentors to learn new skills. This is an experimental program, and we are in the preliminary phase of rolling it out. The program leverages our network and community to connect skilled mentors with motivated learners. Our goal and our hope is to help everyone make the best use of their time at home, and emerge from this crisis stronger, better educated, and more united.

Who is Eligible for this Program
As a mentor, if you have demonstrable skills and work experience in any technical role related to software design and development, and you'd like to share those skills with eager learners, then we'd love to hear from you. As a student, this program is open to people who have recently lost work due to the Covid-19 pandemic, and are committed to self-isolating to flatten the curve. If you are interested in finding a mentor, please Click Here. If you are interested in becoming a mentor, please apply below.

Why We're Launching this Program
Millions of people worldwide are facing dire straits due to workplaces closing during the COVID-19 pandemic. Self-isolation is the most effective way to contain the outbreak, lighten the load on our public health system, and save lives. Yet this leaves many people having to choose between potentially spreading the pandemic vs. forgoing necessary income, while many others are now unemployed due to lock-downs. With everyone hunkered down at home right now, we feel there is no better time to build our community and to help each other learn and grow.

Help Others Learn Tech Skills
Having technical skills will not solve the economic and societal problems we are currently facing, but it can help people to put themselves in a more stable position. Tech jobs are still some of the best paid and high in-demand positions in many places around the world, and they can give people the opportunity to work remotely. Being able to continue creating value and getting paid even during a situation like the current COVID-19 lock-downs is a privilege that we would like to share with affected workers. Join us?
