Solidarity in the times of COVID-19

Economic Instability: Tens of thousands of people find themselves in dire straits through their workplaces being closed down due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Self-isolation is the most effective way to contain the outbreak, lighten the load on our public health system, and save lives. Many economically vulnerably people who are wanting to be respectful, however, find themselves needing to choose between potentially spreading the pandemic or forgoing necessary income. Many don't even have that choice and were simply laid off.

Help Others Learn Tech Skills: Having technical skills will not solve the economic and societal problems we are currently facing, but it can help people to put themselves in a more stable position. Tech jobs are still some of the best paid and high in-demand positions in many places around the world, and they can give you the opportunity to work remotely. Being able to continue creating value and getting paid even during a situation like the current COVID-19 lock-downs is a privilege that we would like to share with affected workers.
